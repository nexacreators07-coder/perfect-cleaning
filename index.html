FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking with Perfect Cleaning.

What areas of London do you cover?
We serve all 32 London boroughs, including Central London, North, South, East, and West London. Areas include Kensington, Chelsea, Westminster, Camden, Canary Wharf, Southwark, and many more. If you are unsure, just ask — we almost certainly cover your area.
Do I need to provide cleaning equipment?
For most services, our cleaners arrive fully equipped with professional-grade tools and eco-friendly products. For regular domestic cleaning, we are happy to use your preferred products if you wish. Just let us know your preference when booking.
Are your cleaners fully insured?
Absolutely. All Perfect Cleaning staff are fully DBS-checked and covered by comprehensive public liability insurance up to 2 million pounds. Your home and belongings are always protected, and you can book with complete confidence.
How do I book a cleaning service?
You can book online using the form above, call us on +44 7456 504021, or message us on WhatsApp for the quickest response. We aim to confirm all bookings within 1 hour and can often accommodate same-day or next-day appointments.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
Yes — we understand life is unpredictable. Simply contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled clean to reschedule at no charge. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a small administration fee.
Do you offer regular cleaning plans?
Yes! Our regular cleaning plans (weekly, fortnightly, or monthly) offer the best value and ensure your home is consistently maintained. Regular clients benefit from priority scheduling, a dedicated cleaner, and preferential rates.
